About El-Ezz Wal Karama

El-Ezz Wal Karama is a community-run nonprofit based on TikTok. We document urgent needs in Gaza, share verified updates, and collect donations that are sent directly to families and local relief partners — with no fees retained by our team.

El-Ezz Wal Karama • Direct donations • Verified reporting

“We pass 100% of donations through to verified recipients in Gaza. We publish receipts and updates so our community can see how help is delivered.”

1) Our Mission

We amplify voices from Gaza through short videos and direct appeals. Our mission is twofold: to raise awareness about humanitarian needs, and to connect donors directly with trusted local partners and families who require immediate assistance.

We operate transparently: every donation is tracked, and we post updates showing how funds are used so supporters can follow the impact of their contributions.

2) How Donations Work

Donations sent through our collection methods are forwarded in full to verified local organizations and community leaders who are actively distributing aid. We never take administrative fees from donated funds.

Verified Partners

We work with local charities and community coordinators who provide proof of delivery and regular reporting.

Tracking & Receipts

We publish summaries and receipts for major transfers so donors can see how funds are used.

3) Our Reporting

Our TikTok videos document conditions and needs on the ground. We strive for accuracy by corroborating reports with local partners and by sharing raw footage and partner statements when possible.

“Every update we publish includes context and sourcing to help viewers make informed decisions.”

4) Transparency & Verification

Transparency is central to our work. We publish summaries of transfers, partner confirmations, and photographic evidence when available. We also explain any limitations or delays openly.

5) How You Can Help

You can support our work by donating, sharing our videos to raise awareness, and by vetting and reporting credible local needs. Even small contributions pooled together make a difference.

We require partner documentation, local references, and corroborating evidence before sending funds.

Yes. Share our content, donate, or reach out via our TikTok DMs for coordination. We prioritize local, on-the-ground collaboration.
